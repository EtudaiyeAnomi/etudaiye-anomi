## 👋 About Me

I am a **Research Assistant** at the **Sheda Science & Technology Complex (SHESTCO), Abuja**, with 3+ years of hands-on laboratory experience at one of Nigeria's premier national research institutions. My work sits at the intersection of **environmental chemistry**, **natural product research**, and **data analytics**.

I hold a **BSc in Industrial Chemistry** from the University of Abuja and am currently advancing my data science skills through the **ALX Africa Data Science Programme**. I am also preparing to begin my **MSc**, continuing my focus on environmental and organic chemistry.

I have authored **two peer-reviewed journal publications** as first author and have presented at **two national scientific conferences**.
